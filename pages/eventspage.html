Student Clubs

The best way to learn about upcoming events is often to look at the student clubs' pages on social media! You can find them here:

Melomanie - The Music Club

Melomanie Club is the student club of DSU for all things musical. Be it vocal talent or instrumental, hindustani classical or western, you'll find it here!

Abhinaya - The Dance Club

DSU is filled with talented dancers trained in dozens of different Indian classical styles as well as Western. Learn more about them here!

Career Trajectory Club

Partnered with ScaleUp USA, The Career Trajectory Club aims to bring the opportunity to study abroad to college students in India, through workshops, courses and expert guidance. Learn more here!

This website was developed by Akash Bagchi and Akshaya Nadathur of Dayananda Sagar University - Kudlu Gate, Bangalore as part of the 4th Semester Web Technologies' Mini Project. The main goal of the website was to create a student portal for CSE students wherein there would be easy access to college material, calendars, announcements etc. thereby removing ambiguity and streamlining the lives of thousands of students. This website uses HTML, CSS, JavaScript and JQuery, Bootstrap4, PHP and MySQL to achieve its functionality. We hope the site served your needs!
